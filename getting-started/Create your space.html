Create Your Space

As we discussed in The Sample Space, the space is your work area and here you work with your team on a project. It will contain the team members, repositories, assets, and execution hosts.

To create a space:

  1. Access the Admin Console.

  2. Click Spaces > Create space and follow the instructions.
    Congratulations! You have created your first space in Torque. Now, connect your asset repository.
